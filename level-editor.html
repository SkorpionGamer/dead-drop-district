Click anything visible to select it. Use `Edit Bounds` to switch between collision and visual boxes. Drag inside moves. Drag the handles to resize. Drag empty space for marquee selection. Wheel zooms. Right or middle drag pans. Smart Add uses the selected asset target. Lamp and floodlight assets route into the Lights layer. Snap Grid locks placement and dragging. Use Rotate buttons or `Q/E` and `R/F` to turn selection. Building Shell mode snaps doors and windows to a selected building grid. `P` toggles playtest, `WASD` moves, `E` toggles doors.
